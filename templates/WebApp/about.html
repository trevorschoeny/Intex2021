Transparency · Reliability · Accessibility

The mission of NOTICe is to fight against the opioid epidemic in the United States by providing a transparent and informative library of knowledge regarding prescription drugs, including opioids, and the physicians across the country who prescribe them.

We believe in accountability and transparency. To that end, we record data for drug prescribers along with every instance in which they prescribe any of the 250 most common drugs.

Read our story

Our founding

In conjunction with the Utah Opioid Task Force ©, NOTICe got to work on providing an accesible database in the form of a web application to allow government officials and prescribers to view the volume of prescriptions that are taking place overall.

Growth & beyond

Looking forward, we aim to become the leading source of information for all things concerning the national opioid epidemic by creating a comprehensive database of every drug prescriber in the nation, along with their prescription tendencies, as well as detailed information about each drug and its possible addictive properties. We plan to empower patients with knowledge about every drug and close substitutes that could be considered before receiving a prescription.
